Build a Consulting Website

Create a simple website advertising your services in areas like web or mobile development, tutoring, data analysis, or app design. Use platforms like Carrd, Wix, or GitHub Pages to get started quickly. To promote your consulting services, consider advertising on platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, LinkedIn, or relevant subreddit communities (e.g., r/freelance, r/webdev). You can also share your offerings in university groups, alumni networks, or local tech meetups.

What to submit: Share the link to your site and include a short paragraph describing the niche you're targeting and how you plan to promote it.

Interview Someone in Your Dream Role

Find someone on LinkedIn or in your extended network working in a role or company you admire. Reach out, request an informational interview, and conduct a 20–30 minute chat. Record and transcribe this interview.

What to submit: A 1-page summary of what you learned and a reflection on how it changes or confirms your career goals. Also include relevant parts of the transcription for verification.

Create a Business Plan for a Tech Startup or Side Hustle

Draft a one-page business plan (Lean Canvas format is fine) for a product, app, or service you could realistically build with your skills.

What to submit: Upload your business plan and a 1-2 paragraph explanation of how this project fits into your long-term goals. Include a brief action plan for getting started.

Reflect on Career Strategy in the Age of GenAI

Write a personal essay exploring how GenAI is reshaping CS jobs, your skills strategy in response, and where you see opportunities and risks. Add a brief next-steps action plan to take advantage of one opportunity.

What to submit: A 1–2 page reflection or blog post.

Post a Technical Tutorial or Deep Dive

Write a high-quality post explaining a technical concept and publish it on Substack, Medium, DEV.to, or similar.

What to submit: A link to your published post and a short summary of why you chose the topic and who the intended audience is.

Join and Participate in a Professional Community

Join an online professional or technical community. Contribute meaningfully to at least one thread.

What to submit: A screenshot or link to your contribution and a brief reflection on what you learned or gained from the interaction.

Conduct a Reverse Job Search

Instead of looking for job postings, list 3 companies or products you admire and explain why. Find a contact at each and write a personalized message or email to each person.

What to submit: The company list and the sample messages that you sent (including contact info at each company). Note: You may need to get creative to find contacts. Try social media, LinkedIn, Google, company directory, etc.

Teach a Workshop or Create a Video Tutorial

Design and deliver a recorded mini-workshop on a CS topic you're comfortable with. Upload this to YouTube.

What to submit: Link to the YouTube video and a brief reflection on how teaching helped solidify your own skills.

Attend a Local or Virtual Tech Meetup or Conference

Attend an event, workshop, or online conference. Engage with at least one new person or speaker.

What to submit: A short reflection on what you learned and who you met. Include a photo or screenshot of your registration and one of your interaction.

LinkedIn Deep Revamp + Outreach

Update your LinkedIn with a compelling summary and projects. Send connection requests with personal notes to 10 professionals or alumni.

What to submit: Link to your updated profile and a brief summary of how your outreach was received.
