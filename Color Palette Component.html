Visual reference for brand colors and design consistency

Preview:

#d73f09 - Beaver Orange
#4A773C - Pine Stand
#003b5c - Star Canvas
#FFB500 - Luminance
#006A8E - Stratosphere

Ingredients:

  • layout: CSS Grid with auto-fit columns
  • min-width: 200px per color sample
  • gap: 10px between samples
  • padding: 15px per sample
  • border-radius: 6px
  • text-align: center
  • font-size: 12pt
  • font-weight: bold
  • text colors: white or black for optimal contrast (#003b5c updated for accessibility)

HTML/CSS Code:

<div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: repeat(auto-fit, minmax(200px, 1fr)); gap: 10px; margin: 1rem 0;">

        #d73f09 - Beaver Orange
    </div>
