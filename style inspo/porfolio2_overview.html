Objectives

  1. Distinguish the variables and characteristics of each inventory models covered in this course.
  2. Create a logic flow chart or decision tree to assist decision maker in choosing the appropriate inventory model.

Project Background

You operate an internal supply shop in your company where anyone can come to you to pick up supplies. Your shop stock any- and everything needed, perishable or not, and some inventories require subassemblies by your staff. You try to manage your own inventory to the best of your ability, however, you occasionally run out of stock as well.

After a year of operation the supply shop, you noticed that you get asked all the time: "How many should I get so that I don't have to come back again until [some later] time?" This gets old. You want to create a simple app (excel + VBA + Macro) where your customer answers a few questions, your app provides the quantity they need, then off they go.

Before you can create the app, you need the logic behind it. This is the focus of this portfolio project.
