Objectives

  1. To perform a forecast and evaluate forecast accuracy using an Excel Spreadsheet.
  2. Provide an assessment of the forecast accuracy and the appropriateness of the forecasting model for the problem context and data structure.
Deadlines
  • Week 2 Monday – Initial post with Excel attachment
  • Week 3 Monday – Two peer responses

Instructions

  1. 1 Step 1: Prepare Your Forecasts

    • Download the data file from Forecasting using Excel Spreadsheet Discussion – Data File and Instructions.
    • Follow the instructions to perform all the required forecasts and evaluations.

  2. 2 Step 2: Submit Your Initial Post (Week 2 Monday)

    • Attach your completed Excel spreadsheet.
    • Respond to the following:
      • Data Set 1:
        • What is your assessment of the two sets of forecasts? Consider the accuracy, consistency, frequency of forecast, and frequency of forecast evaluation (i.e., the one you just did).
      • Data Set 2:
        • What behavior did you observe from the scatter plot?
        • What is your assessment of the linear regression model?
        • Is linear regression appropriate for this set of data? Why or why not?

  3. 3 Step 3: Respond to Peers (Week 3 Monday)

    • Respond to at least two peers. You may consider the following:
      • Look at your peers’ Excel spreadsheet. Identify one to two features that enhance readability/usability and you would like to incorporate into yours.
      • Compare your peer’s assessment of Data Set 1 with your own, identify agreement/disagreement, and discuss those with your peer.
      • Compare your peer’s response to Data Set 2 with your own, identify agreement/disagreement, and discuss those with your peer.
