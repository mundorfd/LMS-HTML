Module Introduction

This module covers fundamental concepts in structural design, focusing on reinforced concrete and wood members. You'll learn to apply design principles and standard terminology in practical structural engineering situations.

Module Learning Outcomes

After successful completion of this module, you will be able to:

  3. MLO 3: text

Course Learning Outcomes

These module outcomes align with the following course-level outcomes:

  • CLO 1: Design simple reinforced concrete members that are controlled by flexure, compression, and shear (beams, columns, and slabs)
  • CLO 2: Design simple wood members that are controlled by flexure, compression, shear, and tension (beams, columns, and trusses)
  • CLO 3: Design simple connections between wood-framed members
  • CLO 4: Explain the standard terminology used in concrete and wood design, and apply it to structural design situations
