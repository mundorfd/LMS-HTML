Warning/Alert Box

Highlighted container for important warnings or critical information

Preview:

Important Warning:

This is critical information that students need to be aware of. It stands out from regular content and draws immediate attention.

Ingredients:

  • background: #FFE6E6 (light red)
  • border: 2px solid #D73F09 (red)
  • border-radius: 6px
  • padding: 1.5rem
  • label color: #D73F09 (matches border)
  • label weight: bold
  • content size: 12pt, line-height 1.6

HTML/CSS Code:

<div style="background: #FFE6E6; border: 2px solid #D73F09; padding: 1.5rem; margin: 1rem 0; border-radius: 6px;">
    <div style="font-weight: bold; font-size: 12pt; margin-bottom: 1rem; color: #D73F09;">Important Warning:</div>
    <div style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 1.6;">
        <p>Your warning content goes here.</p>
    </div>
</div>

Suggested Uses:

  • Course policies and important notices
  • Assignment deadline reminders
  • Safety or academic integrity warnings
  • Critical prerequisite information
  • System or technical requirement alerts
