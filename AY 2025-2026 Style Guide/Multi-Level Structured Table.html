Sophisticated table designs for complex instructional content

Multi-Level Structured Table

Complex table with header rows, grouped sections, and hierarchical content

Preview:

Section Duration Requirements
TEAM INTRODUCTION 1-3 minutes Brief introduction to your team and project. Choose any visuals you like.
FOR EACH TEAM MEMBER (1-3 minutes per member)
INDIVIDUAL OUTLINE Part of individual time Discuss your responsibilities and contributions for the term.
Example subsection: — This shows how to nest examples or additional details under main sections.
TOTAL LENGTH 5-15 minutes Team sections + individual presentations combined

Ingredients:

  • container border: 2px solid #d73f09
  • border-radius: 8px
  • overflow: hidden
  • border-collapse: collapse
  • header background: #d73f09, white text
  • group header background: #00859B (teal), white text
  • group header span: colspan="3" for full width
  • alternating rows: #f9f9f9 background
  • nested examples: #f0f8ff background
  • summary row: #e9e5e4 background
  • indentation: padding-left: 24px (level 1), 48px (level 2)
  • cell padding: 12px
  • cell borders: border-top 1px solid #ccc
  • vertical alignment: top

HTML/CSS Code:

<div style="background: white; border-radius: 8px; overflow: hidden; margin-bottom: 2rem; border: 2px solid #d73f09;">
    <table style="width: 100%; border-collapse: collapse;">
        <thead>
            <tr>
                <th scope="col" style="padding: 12px; background-color: #d73f09; color: white; font-size: 12pt; width: 25%;">Section</th>
                <th scope="col" style="padding: 12px; background-color: #d73f09; color: white; font-size: 12pt; width: 15%;">Duration</th>
                <th scope="col" style="padding: 12px; background-color: #d73f09; color: white; font-size: 12pt;">Requirements</th>
            </tr>
        </thead>
        <tbody>
            <!-- Standard Row -->
            <tr style="background-color: #f9f9f9;">
                <td style="border-top: 1px solid #ccc; padding: 12px; font-size: 12pt; font-weight: bold; vertical-align: top;">Section Name</td>
                <td style="border-top: 1px solid #ccc; padding: 12px; font-size: 12pt; vertical-align: top;">Time</td>
                <td style="border-top: 1px solid #ccc; padding: 12px; font-size: 12pt; vertical-align: top;">Description</td>
            </tr>
            
            <!-- Group Header Row -->
            <tr style="background-color: #00859B; color: white;">
                <td colspan="3" style="border-top: 1px solid #ccc; padding: 12px; font-size: 12pt; font-weight: bold; text-align: center;">
                    GROUP HEADER TEXT
                </td>
            </tr>
            
            <!-- Indented Subsection -->
            <tr>
                <td style="border-top: 1px solid #ccc; padding: 12px; font-size: 12pt; font-weight: bold; vertical-align: top; padding-left: 24px;">Subsection</td>

            <tr style="background-color: #e9e5e4;">
                <td style="border-top: 1px solid #ccc; padding: 12px; font-size: 12pt; font-weight: bold; vertical-align: top;">SUMMARY</td>

                <td style="border-top: 1px solid #ccc; padding: 12px; font-size: 12pt; vertical-align: top;">Summary info</td>
            </tr>
        </tbody>
    </table>
</div>

Suggested Uses:

  • Complex assignment requirements with multiple parts
  • Project presentation guidelines with team and individual components
  • Course schedules with grouped topics and subtopics
  • Assessment rubrics with categories and subcriteria
  • Multi-step processes with nested instructions
