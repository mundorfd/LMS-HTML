    Netflix

    Netflix has one of the most enviable engineering cultures in the software industry. Ever hear of microservices? Netflix invented them.

    Microservices changed the engineering culture at Netflix. Today, every developer runs what they write (often a microservice). They're responsible for fixing their tools if they break, writing the documentation, and dealing with any operations pain they create.

    Core Values

    Freedom and responsibility are at the core of Netflix's business strategy. They attract talent by giving employees the freedom to be creative and flexible, but they only hire responsible people who are worthy of that freedom.

    Some Examples

    Netflix

    Netflix has one of the most enviable engineering cultures in the software industry. Ever hear of microservices? Netflix invented them.

    Microservices changed the engineering culture at Netflix. Today, every developer runs what they write (often a microservice).

    Themes

    Freedom and responsibility are at the core of Netflix's business strategy. They attract talent by giving employees the freedom to be creative and flexible.

    Etsy

    The online arts and crafts marketplace has a very different application architecture compared with Netflix's microservices. Etsy is a monolithic service-oriented architecture with a disciplined continuous delivery process.

    Themes

    Etsy believes engineering processes that make its developers happy are also best for product quality. "Easy deploys = developer happiness," is the motto.

    Theme Color Guide:

    • Beaver Orange (#d73f09): Primary themes, values, core concepts
    • Pine Stand Green (#4A773C): Philosophy, culture, approaches
    • Star Canvas Blue (#003b5c): Quotes, testimonials, external perspectives
    • Stratosphere Blue (#006A8E): Business models, processes, methods
    • Luminance Yellow (#FFB500): Warnings, special notes, highlights
