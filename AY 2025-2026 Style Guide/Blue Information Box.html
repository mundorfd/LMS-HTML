Blue Information Box

Neutral informational container for supplementary content

Preview:

Key Information:

This is supplementary information that provides additional context or helpful details without being critical or warning-level content.

Ingredients:

  • class: "bluebox"
  • border: 3px solid #b8dde1 (light blue)
  • background-color: #feffff (near white)
  • color: #1e1e19 (dark text)
  • padding: 1.5em
  • border-radius: 8px
  • content size: 12pt, line-height 1.6

HTML/CSS Code:

<div class="bluebox" style="border: 3px solid #b8dde1; padding: 1.5em; margin: 1rem 0; background-color: #feffff; color: #1e1e19; border-radius: 8px;">
    <div style="font-weight: bold; font-size: 12pt; margin-bottom: 1rem;">Information Title:</div>
    <div style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 1.6;">
        <p>Your informational content goes here.</p>
    </div>
</div>

Suggested Uses:

  • Course announcements and updates
  • Study tips and learning strategies
  • Supplementary reading recommendations
  • Key takeaways and summaries
  • Background context for lessons
