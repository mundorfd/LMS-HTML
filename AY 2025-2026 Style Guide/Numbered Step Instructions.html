Accessibility Features:

  • aria-hidden="true" on decorative number badges
  • High contrast number badges (AA 2.1 compliant)
  • Semantic heading structure maintained
  • Clear visual hierarchy with consistent spacing
  • Screen reader friendly with proper heading tags

Design Notes:

  • Number badges are purely decorative - step numbers are also in heading text
  • Consistent 8px margin spacing between list items
  • Question boxes use subtle background differentiation
  • Works well with both bulleted lists and paragraph content
