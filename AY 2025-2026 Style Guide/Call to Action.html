Primary Call-to-Action Box

High-impact container for critical actions and assignments

Preview:

Submit Your Final Project

Upload your completed project files to Canvas by 11:59 PM on Friday, July 19th. Late submissions will result in point deductions.

Go to Assignment →

Ingredients:

  • background: #d73f09 (Beaver Orange)
  • color: white text
  • padding: 2rem
  • border-radius: 8px
  • text-align: center
  • heading size: 16pt, bold
  • content size: 12pt, line-height 1.6
  • button background: white
  • button color: #d73f09 (matches container)
  • button padding: 12px 24px

HTML/CSS Code:

<div style="background: #d73f09; color: white; padding: 2rem; margin: 1rem 0; border-radius: 8px; text-align: center;">
    <div style="font-size: 16pt; font-weight: bold; margin-bottom: 1rem;">Your Action Title</div>
    <div style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <p style="margin: 0;">Your description of what needs to be done and any important details or deadlines.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="background: white; color: #d73f09; padding: 12px 24px; border-radius: 6px; display: inline-block; font-weight: bold; font-size: 12pt;">
        Your Button Text →
    </div>
</div>

Suggested Uses:

  • Assignment submission deadlines
  • Registration and enrollment actions
  • Required course surveys or evaluations
  • Important link destinations
  • Next module or lesson navigation

Urgent Call-to-Action Box

High-visibility container for time-sensitive actions

Preview:

⚠️ Deadline Tomorrow

Final day to submit your midterm essay! Don't miss this important deadline. Submit by 11:59 PM tonight to avoid late penalties.

Submit Now →

Ingredients:

  • background: #FDD26E (Candela yellow)
  • border: 3px solid #d73f09 (Beaver Orange)
  • color: black text
  • padding: 2rem
  • border-radius: 8px
  • text-align: center
  • heading size: 16pt, bold
  • content size: 12pt, line-height 1.6
  • button background: #d73f09
  • button color: white
  • warning icon: ⚠️ emoji for urgency

HTML/CSS Code:

<div style="background: #FDD26E; color: black; padding: 2rem; margin: 1rem 0; border-radius: 8px; text-align: center; border: 3px solid #d73f09;">
    <div style="font-size: 16pt; font-weight: bold; margin-bottom: 1rem;">⚠️ Your Urgent Title</div>
    <div style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">
        <p style="margin: 0;"><strong>Your urgent message!</strong> Additional details about the time-sensitive action needed.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="background: #d73f09; color: white; padding: 12px 24px; border-radius: 6px; display: inline-block; font-weight: bold; font-size: 12pt;">
        Your Action Text →
    </div>
</div>

Suggested Uses:

  • Last-minute deadline reminders
  • Course registration deadlines
  • Emergency announcements
  • Time-sensitive survey responses
  • Critical system updates or changes
Usage Note: Use sparingly to maintain impact - urgent styling loses effectiveness when overused
