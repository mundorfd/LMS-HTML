Basic Tip Box

Standard container for helpful tips and advice

Preview:

Where to demo your work? Watch for industry events like shows or conferences or hackathons. This is a natural place to demo your work, or maybe just hand out flyers. At a recent PyCon conference, there were many such official demos happening (but you will have to do some work to be invited).

Ingredients:

  • background: #f0f8ff (light blue)
  • border: 2px solid #006A8E (blue)
  • border-radius: 6px
  • padding: 1.5rem
  • content size: 12pt, line-height 1.6
  • emphasis: bold for key points

HTML/CSS Code:

<div style="background: #f0f8ff; border: 2px solid #006A8E; padding: 1.5rem; margin: 1rem 0; border-radius: 6px;">
    <div style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 1.6;">
        <p style="margin: 0;"><strong>Your tip title or question?</strong> Your helpful tip content goes here.</p>
    </div>
</div>

Suggested Uses:

  • Study strategies and learning techniques
  • Assignment completion tips
  • Resource recommendations
  • Time management advice
  • Course navigation guidance

Pro-Tip Box with Nested Highlight

Advanced tip container with highlighted professional advice

Preview:

Where to demo your work? Watch for industry events like shows or conferences or hackathons. This is a natural place to demo your work, or maybe just hand out flyers. At a recent PyCon conference, there were many such official demos happening (but you will have to do some work to be invited).

Networking Pro Tip: Don't ask directly for a job. Ask for feedback, advice, or info on your work. This should always be a casual conversation.

Ingredients:

  • outer background: #f0f8ff (light blue)
  • outer border: 2px solid #006A8E (blue)
  • outer border-radius: 6px
  • outer padding: 1.5rem
  • nested background: #006A8E (blue)
  • nested color: white text
  • nested padding: 1rem
  • nested border-radius: 6px
  • nested margin-top: 1rem
  • nested font-style: italic

HTML/CSS Code:

<div style="background: #f0f8ff; border: 2px solid #006A8E; padding: 1.5rem; margin: 1rem 0; border-radius: 6px;">
    <div style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 1.6;">
        <p style="margin: 0;"><strong>Your main tip or question?</strong> Your primary tip content goes here.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="background: #006A8E; color: white; padding: 1rem; margin-top: 1rem; border-radius: 6px;">
        <div style="font-size: 12pt; font-style: italic;">
            <p style="margin: 0;"><strong>Pro Tip:</strong> Your advanced professional advice goes here.</p>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>

Suggested Uses:

  • Advanced study techniques with expert advice
  • Career guidance with professional insights
