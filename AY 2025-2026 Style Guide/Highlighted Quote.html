Primary Theme Box (Beaver Orange)

High-impact container for core concepts and key themes

Preview:

Team-first Company Culture

Team-oriented companies hire for culture fit first, skills and experience second.

At Team-oriented companies, employee engagement is the top priority. These companies may hold frequent team outings, opportunities to provide meaningful feedback and flexibility to accommodate employees' family.

Service-oriented companies often embrace Team-first company culture. This type of culture normally makes for happy employees, and happy employees will give better customer service.

Ingredients:

  • container background: #fafafa (light gray)
  • container padding: 2rem
  • container border-radius: 8px
  • heading border-bottom: 1px solid #e6e7e8
  • heading size: 14pt
  • theme box background: #d73f09 (Beaver Orange)
  • theme box color: white text
  • theme box padding: 1rem
  • theme box border-radius: 6px
  • theme box margin: 0 (removes bottom margin)
  • content size: 11pt, line-height 1.6

HTML/CSS Code:

<div style="margin-bottom: 3rem; padding: 2rem; background: #fafafa; border-radius: 8px;">
    <h3 style="border-bottom: 1px solid #e6e7e8; font-size: 14pt; margin: 0 0 1rem 0;">Your Section Title</h3>
    <div style="background: #d73f09; color: white; padding: 1rem; border-radius: 6px; margin-bottom: 1rem;">
        <h4 style="font-size: 12pt; margin: 0;"><strong>Your key theme or main point here.</strong></h4>
    </div>
    <div style="font-size: 11pt; line-height: 1.6; color: #333333;">
        <p style="margin: 0 0 1rem 0;">First paragraph of supporting content that explains the theme in more detail.</p>

    </div>
</div>

Suggested Uses:

  • Core learning objectives and key concepts
  • Primary themes and main ideas
  • Critical takeaways and summaries
  • Important quotes from authorities
  • Company mission statements or values

Pine Stand (Green) - Secondary Themes:

Elite Corporate Culture

Companies with elite cultures are out to change the world.

An elite corporate culture hires only the best and brightest. Elite companies are pushing the envelope and need employees to not merely keep up, but lead the way (think Google). These are innovative and daring companies who hire creative, confident, capable, and competitive candidates.

Star Canvas (Dark Blue) - Money Quotes:

Facebook Engineering

Money quote:

"Fast doesn't just win the race. It gets a head start for the next one."

Facebook's approach emphasizes speed and continuous momentum. This philosophy drives their development practices and competitive strategy in the fast-moving tech landscape.

Color Usage Guide:

  • Beaver Orange (#d73f09): Primary themes, core concepts, main ideas
  • Pine Stand Green (#4A773C): Secondary themes, supporting concepts
  • Star Canvas Blue (#003b5c): Quotes, testimonials, external perspectives
  • Luminance Yellow (#FFB500): Warnings, highlights, special notes (uses black text for accessibility)
  • Stratosphere Blue (#006A8E): Processes, methods, implementation details

Suggested Uses:

  • Content hierarchy with color coding
  • Theme categorization in lessons
  • Quote highlighting and attribution
  • Key concept reinforcement
  • Process step explanations
Accessibility Note: All color combinations provide sufficient contrast for AA compliance. Luminance yellow uses black text (11.86:1 ratio) instead of white for optimal readability
